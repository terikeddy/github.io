Frequently Asked Questions

Your Burning Questions, Answered!

How often will I meet with my coach?

The frequency of coaching sessions depends on your individual needs and goals. Typically, clients meet once a week or biweekly to ensure steady progress.

What if I need to reschedule a coaching session?

Life happens! Simply notify me at least 24 hours in advance, and we'll find a time that works best for you.

How long does the coaching relationship typically last?

Coaching is a journey that varies for each individual. Most clients see significant progress within 3 to 6 months, but the relationship can be extended as needed.

Do you offer virtual coaching sessions?

Yes, all sessions can be conducted virtually, allowing you to receive coaching from the comfort of your home or wherever you are.

Can I customize my coaching package?

Absolutely! My coaching is tailored to fit your specific needs, so you can adjust the frequency and focus of sessions as needed.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your session with a 24-hour notice. No-shows or late cancellations may result in a fee.
