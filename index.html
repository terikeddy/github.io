Life with ADHD Is Hard — Accountability Doesn’t Have to Be.

Helping You Stay on Track and Consistent, One Week at a Time.

Start Showing Up for Yourself, Today

Hey, I’m Teri, and I’m here to help you stay on track.

If you’re here, I’m guessing you’re tired of feeling stuck in a cycle of knowing what you need to do—but not being able to follow through.

I’ve been there. I’ve been there. As an AuADHD woman, I spent years battling myself—trying to follow through on the things I truly cared about. It’s frustrating. It’s exhausting. And, honestly? It sucks.

That’s why I created this accountability program. I know how much easier it is to move forward when you’ve got someone in your corner, offering support, structure, and a little extra push when you need it.

You don’t have to fight through this alone. Together, we’ll find a way to make progress feel manageable—and even achievable.

My Mission

I’m here to help people with ADHD feel less alone in the struggle and more capable of moving forward.

My mission is to provide thoughtful structure so you can practice consistency, encouragement so you can take action, and a roadmap for progress so you can keep moving forward—tailored to fit your unique challenges and strengths.

No judgment, no gimmicks—just the support you need to start making real, meaningful progress.

My Core Values

  • Empathy: I’ve been where you are, and I know how tough it can be. That’s why I approach every client with understanding, patience, and zero judgment.
  • Accountability Without Shame: I believe in providing structure and support that feels encouraging—not punishing.
  • Authenticity: This program isn’t about perfection or cookie-cutter solutions. It’s about figuring out what works for you.
  • Consistency: Small, steady wins build real progress. I’m here to help you stick with it—even on the tough days.
