How I Can Help

Accountability That Meets You Where You Are

If you’ve been telling yourself, “I’ll figure it out eventually,” but nothing ever seems to change, you’re not alone—and you don’t have to do this alone either.

This isn’t just another cookie-cutter productivity program. My Signature Service is about helping you build momentum and consistency in a way that feels manageable, not overwhelming.

I’m here to offer real, tangible support—someone to check in with, celebrate your wins, and help you navigate the tough days. We’ll tackle the things that feel impossible, break them into small, achievable steps, and move forward one week at a time.

This program is built for people like you:

  • Who know what they need to do but feel stuck on how to get started.
  • Who are tired of trying to hold themselves accountable without success.
  • Who need a partner—not a drill sergeant.

You’re not failing because you’re not trying hard enough. You’re struggling because ADHD brains thrive with external accountability—and that’s exactly what I’m here to provide.

Let’s move forward together.

How It Works

Getting started doesn’t have to feel overwhelming. Together, we’ll create a system that works for you—one step at a time.

01

Monthly Kickoff Session

Start each month with a brain dump and a prioritized plan. Together, we’ll break your goals into manageable weekly steps.

02

Weekly Planning Sessions

Review your wins, adjust your plan, and set clear priorities for the week in a focused 30-minute session.

03

Daily Check-Ins

Quick, flexible updates through private chat to celebrate wins, troubleshoot challenges, or ask for guidance.

04

SOS & Mulligan Days

Use SOS sessions for real-time support or Mulligan days to reset and refocus when life feels overwhelming.

Monthly Investment

This is more than accountability—it’s a partnership designed to help you make consistent progress without the overwhelm.

Monthly Investment Includes:

  • Monthly Kickoff Session: A 1-hour session to brain dump, prioritize tasks, and set clear goals for the first week. Includes a personalized to-do list and follow-up notes.
  • Weekly Planning Sessions: Three 30-minute sessions each month to review wins, challenges, and priorities. Updated action plans are sent after each session.
  • Daily Check-Ins: Quick, low-pressure check-ins via a personal chat room for updates, questions, and encouragement. Choose the 5 days per week that work best for you.
  • Mulligan Days: Life happens—use your 2 Mulligan Days anytime, no explanation needed. Just text “Mulligan,” and we’ll reconnect the next day.
  • SOS Sessions: Two on-demand support sessions per month for urgent needs, body doubling, or a brain dump. Real-time support (chat, voice, or video) with a guaranteed 24-hour response.
  • End-of-Month Progress Report: A personalized summary of wins, challenges, and accomplishments to reflect and plan for the next month.
