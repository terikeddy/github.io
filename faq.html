Frequently Asked Questions

Your Burning Questions, Answered!

What is an Accountability Partner?

An Accountability Partner is someone who provides hands-on support, regular check-ins, and positive pressure to help you stay on track. I’m here to guide you, hold you accountable, and celebrate your wins—especially when ADHD makes it hard to follow through on your own.

How is this different from ADHD coaching?

Unlike traditional ADHD coaches, I’m not here to “fix” you or give one-size-fits-all advice. As your Accountability Partner, I work alongside you, focusing on practical, actionable support tailored to your needs.

What if I miss a check-in or can't follow through one day?

Life happens, especially with ADHD. That’s why I’ve built Mulligan Days into the program—no-judgment days where you can take a break if needed. We’ll reconnect and get back on track.

DWhat are SOS sessions?

SOS sessions are on-demand, real-time support sessions where you can call in extra help when you’re stuck. Whether you need a body double or a brain dump, I’m here to provide that push.

Is there a long-term commitment?

No! The program is month-to-month with a 30-day cancellation policy. Payments are upfront, and there are no refunds, ensuring full commitment for the month you’ve paid for.

How quickly can I get started?

We can usually get started within a week of your free consultation. Once you sign up, we’ll schedule your kickoff session to dive into planning and goal-setting.
